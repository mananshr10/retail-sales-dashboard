# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

Designed and developed an end-to-end Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard that transforms 
1,000 real transactions into clear, actionable business insights for smarter decision-making.

📊 Dashboard Highlights:
✅ Sales & Revenue Performance Tracking  
✅ Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis (2023)  
✅ Category-wise Sales Breakdown (Electronics, Clothing, Beauty)  
✅ Gender-wise & Age Group Revenue Insights  
✅ KPI Cards — Total Revenue, Avg Order Value, Transactions & Top Category  

🔍 Key Insights:
🔸 Total revenue of ₹4,56,000 across 1,000 transactions  
🔸 Electronics emerged as the top-performing category at ₹1,56,905  
🔸 May 2023 was the peak revenue month at ₹53,150  
🔸 The 46–60 age group contributed the highest revenue (~32% of total)  
🔸 Female customers led in Beauty & Clothing; Males led in Electronics
